About Me

Hi, I'm Sia, a full stack web developer based in California, USA. I have a Masters in Healthcare Administration, but my passion for web development led me to pursue a career in this field. I have experience working with a variety of programming languages and tools, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and Node.js. Outside of work, I enjoy hiking and exploring the beautiful California landscape. I believe that good communication, attention to detail, and a strong work ethic are the keys to success in any project, and I strive to embody these qualities in all aspects of my life.

Work

I've had the opportunity to work on some exciting projects throughout my career, including creating a responsive e-commerce website for a local business and developing a user-friendly web application for a healthcare organization. These projects have helped me hone my skills in both front-end and back-end development, as well as in database management. In addition to my Masters degree, I hold a certificate in full stack web development from UC Irvine, which has helped me stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in the field. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and techniques to improve my work and provide the best possible experience for users.
